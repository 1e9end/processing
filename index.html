Infected

March 2020 (14yo)

1400 lines of JS

Asteroids

July 2019 (13yo)

550 lines of JS

Remake of the famous arcade game Asteroids. Complete with particles, menus, graphics & effects. Original version had audio, but was removed during porting. First time applying trigonometry in a game.
